### Week 4 Objectives:

* Deepen the theoretical understanding of VPC Peering and AWS Transit Gateway for multi-VPC architectures.
* Learn how Route 53 Resolver enables Hybrid DNS between on-premises and AWS.
* Learn the basics of AWS Site-to-Site VPN and CloudFormation for Infrastructure as Code.

### Week 4 Achievements:

* Understood how **VPC Peering** connects two VPCs directly and where its routing limitations lie (no transitive peering).

* Understood how **AWS Transit Gateway** simplifies networking for multiple VPCs by acting as a central hub.

* Learned the role of **Route 53 Resolver** — Inbound and Outbound Endpoints plus Resolver Rules — in connecting on-premises DNS with AWS.

* Reviewed the fundamentals of **AWS Site-to-Site VPN** and **AWS CloudFormation**, setting up the knowledge needed for the upcoming hands-on labs.

### Plan for Next Week:

* Put this networking theory into practice: deploy VPC Peering, Transit Gateway, Hybrid DNS with Route 53 Resolver, and a Site-to-Site VPN lab, plus revisit AWS Budgets for cost management.
